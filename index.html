To my love,

Dear Babe, this ongoing story of our love is something I never could have expected to be a part of. I relate to the saying that the best love is the unexpected one, and that's exactly how our story began. When we got matched on Bumble, I wasn't really expecting anything to happen but I had a feeling that there was something special about you. During our chatting there, I was drawn to your humor, and I got a feeling that you were a kind and warm person so I was really hoping that you won't be someone that will just ghost me after a few messages. Before asking you out on a date, I was really nervous and I was overthinking that maybe I might do too much and overwhelm you, but deep inside me I am getting the feeling that you might be okay with it. Sadly it didn't go through because I got into an accident which made me even more nervous because you might actually ghost me which I totally understand but you took the risk and waited for me to be able to walk again and ask you out again. That was the moment I knew that you are someone really special and that I started to develop feelings for you. I could never forget the first time I saw you, your kinda messy hair, your shy and nervous smile (you were nervous that you might turn into human meat hahhaaha), your ghost fighter shirt (which I never saw again, asa naman to??), your jeans and your bag. My heart skipped a beat (g kilig ko hehe) when you said you lied to your mom that you went to a friend's house or was it going to the office? I already told you this a thousand times but I will say it again, it was the moment I fell in love with you. You were very beautiful that day. I will never forget that you also took a risk for me by waiting for me to be able to walk normally. It took me 3 months to be okay and you really waited and I am very grateful for that. I hope that until know, the risk you did was worth it. the way my heart In my field, there's a famous saying that "Change is the only constant", I don't relate to that on a personal level because you are my only constant, my safe space, my comfort and confidant and my greatest treasure. I became a became more aware of how important it is to communicate openly and honestly, to support each other's dreams, and to find joy in the little things. I know I still have a long way to go especially about communicating my feelings and thoughts but hopefully I am improving. Because of you, I've become a better version of myself and I will keep striving to become a better version of myself. As we celebrate this milestone, I want you to know that my love for you grows deeper with each breath. You are my present and my future. To us babe, to our past, our present, and the future we'll write together. Forever Yours
Your Love, AJ
